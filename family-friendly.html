Child, Family and Dog Friendly

The Bell Inn at Chittlehampton is a child, dog and family friendly village pub. We have two patio areas, one overlooking the lawns, both with seating.

Children can enjoy themselves in the large sunny garden containing play equipment with plenty of room to run off all that energy while you enjoy a quiet drink! Adjoining the garden is an orchard.

The Bell's beer garden and orchard back onto fields currently occupied by horses, cows and sheep. A public footpath runs through the field and you can enjoy wonderful countryside views.

Children can play on the swings or anywhere in the grassy play area.
