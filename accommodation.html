Spacious and comfortable bed & breakfast and self catering accommodation are available at The Bell.

New for 2016 - We now have a brand new 1 bedroom self catering apartment!

The apartment, contained within the Bell, is perfect for those looking to holiday in the lovely North Devon countryside or for business travellers.

The apartment includes the following features:

  • Open plan seating/dining area with views over Chittlehampton square and church
  • Brand new sofa
  • Large flat screen TV with Freeview channels
  • Dining table with chairs
  • Fully equipt kitchen with oven and all cutlery provided
  • Bedroom with large double bed and views over the countryside
  • En suite bathroom with shower.

The apartment is £60 per night but discounts are available for longer bookings.

To book our apartment room or for more information, please contact us or you can book direct on the airbnb website.

Other rooms available

There is one double room with en suite bathroom (including shower), one twin room with en suite shower and another double room with shower and private toilet.

The fabulous and spacious double room at the front overlooks the village square and St Heiritha's Parish Church. There is a spacious three piece en suite bathroom, colour TV and tea and coffee making facilities.

The double room situated at the rear of the pub has a shower, private toilet and tea and coffee making facilities. This room also provides wonderful countryside views.

Our twin room also located at the rear of the pub offers an en suite shower plus tea and coffee making facilities and wonderful countryside views.

All rooms have:

  • tea and coffee making facilities
  • a colour television
  • en suite with shower.
