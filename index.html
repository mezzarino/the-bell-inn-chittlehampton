Mark, Lynn, Matthew & Kyran welcome you to their child and dog friendly traditional local North Devon pub.

The pub has been with the same family since 1975 when David and Susan Reed purchased the Bell. Susan's middle daughter Lynn and her husband Mark came to run the pub in September 1988.

Mark and Lynn have now been joined by their sons Matthew and Kyran, who wish to build on the strong unique selling points of the Bell: great value, great food (locally sourced) and great real ales with a fantastic atmosphere which is hard to beat.

We are dedicated in bringing you top quality food at great prices with individually prepared meals using only local butchers and fresh farm produce. Mark and Lynn are sure the mouth-watering fayre at The Bell has something for everyone including a great Kids Menu. View our great menus here.

We have been recommended by the Food & Drink Guide, The UK’s leading independent restaurant reviewer.

The Bell offers spacious bed & breakfast and self catering accommodation. Perfect if you are planning a holiday in North Devon or if you are in the area on a business trip.

There is a large garden at the rear with a play area for children next to an orchard. All of this is set in the heart of the beautiful North Devon Countryside.

If you are planning a party, banquet, reception, business conference or any other social event, we have a function room that can cater for up to 70 guests. We also offer an outside bar service. We can cater for any event in South West England.

Although we are a non-smoking pub, we do have a smoking area. And there is free Wi-Fi internet access. Not to mention the Shove ha'penny, skittle alley, pool and darts! All in the setting of a traditional English village pub bar with modern facilities such as a dining conservatory and easy disabled access.
